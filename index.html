BEING A STRANGER

When you become a Stranger you're acquiring a direct pass to experiences and benefits that will be unlocked over time (see ROAD MAP). Strangers do different things that change the world.

Stranger Dudes in addition to being a society full of art and community, is the beginning of what will become a high-value fashion design brand that seeks to use blockchain technology to provide future benefits to the owners of each NFT, such as early access to drops and exclusive products for members.
